Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android application Pariwar Agro Store, developed and maintained by TechXSpire.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and store the following types of information from users:

  • Full Name
  • Phone Number
  • Delivery Address (including Pincode, City, Taluka, District)
  • Order Details (product name, quantity, total cost, etc.)
  • Farming-related Information (crops grown, land area owned)

2. Purpose of Data Collection

The information collected is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To register users and manage accounts
  • To process and deliver product orders
  • To maintain a record of user orders and purchase history
  • To provide agro-based recommendations and support
  • To improve our service offerings based on farming needs

3. Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored securely using Google Firebase services, including Firebase Realtime Database and Firebase Authentication. We ensure that only authorized personnel within TechXSpire have access to this data, and we take all reasonable measures to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third-party organizations or companies. All information is used internally by TechXSpire solely for app functionality and service improvement.

5. Security Measures

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. However, we are committed to ensuring your information remains as safe as possible.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
